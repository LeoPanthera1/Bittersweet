Place the .plt into the palette-library folder of your shot.

To access it, open up your shot. Then in the Colour tab, click the View Menu button (the upper left grey button), and select Palettes> Link...

Find the Bittersweet_Color_Palette.plt and then hit Okay.

A new Scene Palette has been created for you! To access your default Palette or the Bittersweet one, just hit the Show Palette List View button (the Right-most button with two arrows in the Colour tab).
